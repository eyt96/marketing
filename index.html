What is CROW Marketplace?

CROW marketplace is where you are able to purchase your goods securely and safely without the worry of not being the refunded a purchased item online that you didnt receive. Simply pay through the blockchain and verify it has been received on your end! Simply purchase and a smart contract agreement will be made between the seller and you!

Paying with CROW

In our marketplace there is only one currency that we are invested in, and that is Etheruem! Simply pay through the meta-mask extension with Ethereum currency and it will securely go through our Blockchain! How the payment works is that the agreement between the buyer and seller is kept by a third party which is our blockchain until the specific CONDITIONS are met in the smart contract agreement. After the order is verified to be received, the payment will go through to the seller and the collaterals that both the buyer and seller have given will be returned!

What are Collaterals?

What makes CROW marketplace unique is where we ensure that both ends of the agreement are always met! Collaterals are calculated based on the item price that is listed on the CROW marketplace. Every time a smart contract agreement is made between the buyer and seller, a collateral is required to be paid by both. This collateral encourages both ends to do their parts to complete the smart contract agreement.

What is Blockchain

  • Blockchain

    Blockchain is a digital ledger of transactions that records information in a way that makes it nearly impossible to hack or cheat the system, making it a verifiable and auditable record of every transaction.

  • Network

    The blockchain network is where to digital ledger of transactions is distributed over the entire network. The Distributed Ledger Technology is a decentralised database which is managed by multiple participants.

  • Proof of Work

    All or any validated records are not able to be changed and are irrevisible. All network participants have a copy of the ledger for complete transperency. So all information that goes through the blockchain are secure and all the records are individually encrypted.
